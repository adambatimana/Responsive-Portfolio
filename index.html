About Me

Hello my name is Adam Batimana and welcome to my portfolio page. I have had some experience with coding and in the portfolio tab I have links that send you to these coding projects. I have done JavaScript game and responsive websites.


Some of my outside of coding hobbies include personal photography, Jiu Jitsu, and adventuring


I just moved to New Jersey from San Diego, CA. I was a Junior Graphic Designer for a company called Balboa Manufacturing. Previous to that I was a RADAR operator on P-3 Orions for the United States Navy.


My position as a Junior Graphic Designer I was in charge of creating marketing project for the Sales department to use. I edited photos and created flyers and email blasts to be sent out as well.
